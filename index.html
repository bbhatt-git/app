About SARC Academic Club

Fostering scholastic discipline, research aptitude, and collaborative learning.

The SARC Academic Club is a student-run organization dedicated to creating a vibrant community for students passionate about academic excellence, critical research, and collaborative learning.

Our Mission

To provide a platform for students to excel in their scholastic pursuit, enhance critical thinking and presentation skills, and connect with experts in various academic fields.

What We Do

We organize inter-disciplinary quizzes, debate competitions, paper writing seminars, and guest lectures. Our goal is to deepen theoretical understanding and apply knowledge effectively.
